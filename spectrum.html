Electromagnetic Spectrum

Electromagnetic fields are areas of energy that surround electronic devices. Electric fields are created by differences in voltage and the higher the voltage the stronger will be the field. Magnetic fields is created when electric current flows and the greater the current, the dtronger the magnetic field. Electric field is present always, even when the electric current does not flow.
