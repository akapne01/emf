Planets

In Vedic astrology planets are called Grahas and this world in translation from Sanscrit language means seizing. Vedic astrology is only considering 2 luminaries: Sun and Moon, 5 visible planets: Mars, Mercury, Jupiter, Venus, Saturn and 2 nodes of the Sun and Moon called Rahu or South Node and Ketu or North node. They are the eclipse points between the orbits of the Sun and Moon.

Luminaries

Luminaries are Sun and Moon. In astrology they are considered to be planets so I will be calling them planets. Only because of the Sun and Moon life on Earth is able to exist. In the sense these 2 are the most important planets in astrology and also on Earth. Because of the Sun we have a light and Moon is responsible for all nature cycles.
